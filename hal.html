Digital Fleet Map

Problem

Given that Holland America Group's existing digital fleet map is scaled for large landscape displays, how do we translate it to mobile, tablet, and laptop views for users to easily interact with?


Existing Platform: Insight

Holland America Group's Insight is a digital product that maps the live location and details of each cruise in the four brands of Holland America Group. The information presented includes the ship time, latitude and longitude, speed, temperature, wind speed, webcam picture, the departure and destination ports, and social media updates. This product is presented on a large wall display, giving it the ability to hold all these information without making the interface feel cluttered.




Research

To explore the problem and gain inspiration, we searched for existing digital fleet maps on the market. Two of the existing maps we found are MarineTraffic and VesselFinder, both of which are web resources that provide details on vessels around the world and map them accordingly. Existing resources like these helped determine the scope of our project, including what features we should and shouldn't design. Research also helped us consider how we should present our information and design user interactions to ensure that the interface is easy to use and information is not overwhelming.


Goals

After research and defining our scope, we came up with the following goals with our stakeholders:

  • Ability for guests to locate all cruises in the same brand
  • Ability to learn facts about different ships and ports
  • Maintain existing design style defined by the brand
  • Ensure for consistency, usability, and an exceptional UX
