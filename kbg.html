Introduction

From January to October 2020, I worked as a UX Designer for Kabbage's mobile app team, where I was involved in a variety of Kabbage's financial technology products' mobile presence. In this role, I helped align the mobile app to the web experience while devising unique features to enhance the user experience of the native app. The project discussed in this case study has not been released to the public yet, please reach out to me and I'd be happy to discuss what I can about this project.
