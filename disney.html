Introduction

In this assigment done for my Visual Information Design class, I created an infographic from scratch using Adobe Illustrator. I translated the visual potential of appearance, time, relationship, time, numbers, and location into the design. Additionally, I incorporated the principles of visual hierarchy, unity, color, and typography to effectively communciate information to viewers.
